About Plants

Some interesting facts about plants.

Some plants like the Mimosa pudica, or know as the "sensitive plant" can fold its leaves into itself when touches. The Venus flytrap is another good example of plants like this, where as the fly will land on the plant. The plant will then shut closed to trap its prey.

Trees can live for thousands of years, like the bristelcome pine tree can live for over 4,000 years.
